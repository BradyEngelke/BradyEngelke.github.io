Welcome! This portfolio showcases the art of data storytelling through my creative work as well as the books that have served as my inspiration. Data science allows an analyst to formulate statistically justifiable answers to interesting questions within nearly any industry. Inherently, data science is a complex endeavor, but when care is taken to craft a compelling story accompanied by intuitive graphics, technical statistics can transform into powerful business initiatives.

A quick note about me, I obtained an MS in Business Analytics from the University of Minnesota - Carlson School of Management in May 2020 and a BS in Industrial Engineering degree from Iowa State University in December 2018. Along the way, I have provided analytical support to the engineers at Walt Disney World, consulted the CDO of Hennepin County & CMO of Principal Financial Group, and spent a summer as an entry-level consultant for Rockwell Automation.
