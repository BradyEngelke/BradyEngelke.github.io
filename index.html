Welcome! I constructed this page to showcase the art of data-storytelling through my own creative work from a data journalist perspective as well as the books that have served as my inspiration. Data science allows an analyst to formulate statistically justifiable answers to interesting questions that can impact business decisions within nearly any industry. Inherently, data science is a complex endeavor, but when care is taken to craft a compelling story accompanied with intuitive graphics, technical statistics can transform into powerful business initiatives.

A quick note about me, I obtained my Industrial Engineering degree from Iowa State University in December of 2018. I am now studying to earn a Master of Science in Business Analytics from the University of Minnesota - Carlson School of Management. Along the way, I have provided analytical support to the engineers at Walt Disney World, consulted the CMO of Principal Financial Group, and spent a summer as an entry-level consultant for Rockwell Automation.
